Task: Unsupervised Machine Learning:

This Machine Learning Clustering algorithm shows three clusters:
1) Countries near the equator have a volatile level of irradiance, some have a lot, some have a little, likely due to humidity within those climates that causes cloud-cover.
2) Countries in the 2000km-4000Km area vary much less in their level of irradiance and have, on average, higher irradiance.
3) Countries far from the equator, 4000+Km either way, vary less than those near the equator but, on average, have a lower irradiance.
This furthers the belief that countries around the tropics of capricorn and cancer host the best average irradiance in the world among all the countries and thus are the best places for solar energy adoption
